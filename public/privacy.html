What Do You Do With My Information?

At The Sipping Room ("We", "Us", or "Our"), we respect your privacy and take protecting it seriously. This Privacy Policy covers our collection, use, and disclosure of information we collect through our website and service, http://www.thesippingroom.com, owned and operated by The Tea Company, LLC.

The Sipping Room is committed to protecting the privacy of its members. Our site has been designed and built with our members' privacy and security front in mind. These principles guide our privacy policy:

We employ other companies and people to perform tasks on our behalf and need to share your information with them to provide certain services to you, such as shipping and credit card processing. We also are affiliated with a variety of businesses, including tea plantations, and a cafe whose products may be sold to you through our website. We may need to share some of your personal information with them, but these companies do not retain, share, store or use personally identifieable information beyoned what is necessary to assist us.

Registration is required in order to use this website and a user must provide Personal Information prior to using our service. Personal Information includes such items as name, email, phone number, and shipping address and financial information such as credit card number, expiration date, billing address, and shipping address. This information is necessary in order for us to contact users to confirm membership, and to facilitate shipping and billing.

When our signup form asks users to enter sensitive information (such as credit card number), that information is encrypted with SSL (Secure Socket Layers) encryption. Our third party e-commerce providers, who manage billing and payment processing for our site, also use SSL encryption technology.
